Behind the Scenes

We trekked across volcanic terrain at sunrise to capture the aurora’s edge. Motion-activated LEDs were synchronized with drone choreography, illuminating garments as the models moved.

Photographer Kyra Sol and cinematographer Daeon Li used long-exposure techniques to render trails of light around each silhouette.
