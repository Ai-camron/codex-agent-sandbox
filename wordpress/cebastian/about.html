About CEBASTIAN

Forged Between Myth & Metropolis

CEBASTIAN Co. is a luxury streetwear house built on the belief that legends live among us. We weave folklore into modern silhouettes, championing craftsmanship, inclusivity, and sustainability.

Origin Story

Our founder, Sebastian Cruz, grew up between Bogotá and Brooklyn, sketching armor inspired by Andean myths and cyberpunk cinema. CEBASTIAN launched in 2017 with a self-funded micro drop that sold out overnight. Today, we operate a hybrid studio that blends couture tailors, textile scientists, and digital artists crafting AR-enhanced garments.

Every capsule is a narrative arc—prologues release in our newsletter, chapters unfold with each drop, and epilogues live in immersive experiences.

Design Philosophy

We call it mythic utility. Expect hidden pockets, reinforced seams, and ergonomic patterning disguised within sculptural forms. Our atelier iterates on 3D prototypes, stress-tests in VR, then crafts the final garments by hand.

  • • Laser-cut panels aligned with human kinetic maps.
  • • Integrated RFID tags for authenticity and story unlocks.
  • • Vegan leather alternatives sourced from cactus fiber.

Sustainability

Guardians of the Realm

Our sustainability strategy is rooted in reciprocity—we give back to the lands and communities that inspire our stories.

Circular Fabric Lab

We upcycle production offcuts into modular accessories and donate surplus to youth design programs.

Carbon Neutral Logistics

Shipments are offset via verified reforestation initiatives in Colombia and Iceland.

Ethical Supply Chain

Our partners are audited annually to ensure fair wages, safe workshops, and inclusive hiring.
